About Me

As a little girl, Daniela always dreamed of competing on the Miss California stage. Born and raised in the Golden State, Daniela loves the natural beauty of her home, and spends time hiking and appreciating the great outdoors. Other hobbies of Daniela's include cooking and dancing. From her love of performing, she found an affinity for modeling, and it wasn't long before she made the leap to pageants. In 2014 she was Miss Oakland Lantina, and placed in the top five for Miss California Latina. This year she hopes to represent Central California at the Miss California pageant. If she is given this opportunity she will use her platform to promote engineering for young women in low income communities.
