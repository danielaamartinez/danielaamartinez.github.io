The Miss San Joaquin County Scholarship Organization is a non-profit organization and an official preliminary to the Miss California and Miss America competitions. By winning a title on February 9th, not only will I be participating in local community service programs and events representing our area, but I will also be competing at the next level, Miss California, in June.

How can you help?
Sponsoring me by placing a support ad in our competition program book will increase the scholarship dollars that I will earn, in addition to providing you recognition in our community. Ads are available in various sizes and price ranges. Proceeds will benefit the Miss Central California Scholarship Fund.
